Recipe(s)

  1. Home-made Omelette

    2. Ingredients:

    1. 3 Eggs
    2. Slice of bacon (Optional)
    3. Hot dog sausage (Optional)
    4. Shredded Cheese (Optional but sad if you don't have it)
    5. Bowl

    Step 1: Cut up the hot dog into slices and cook it alongside the bacon.

    Step 2: Prepare the eggs by cracking them into a bowl and whisking until mostly homogenous.

    Step 3: Set the bacon and sausage aside and dump the eggs into the pan. Tilt the pan so that the eggs are largely evenly spread over the pan

    Step 4: Top the eggs with the bacon, sausage and cheese to taste.

    Step 5: Once the eggs are solid, fold in into thirds. The great part about this step is that if you mess up, you can just pivot into scrambled eggs.

    Step 6: Plate and serve.
